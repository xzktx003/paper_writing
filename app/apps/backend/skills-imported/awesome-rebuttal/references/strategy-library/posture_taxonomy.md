# Rebuttal Posture Taxonomy

- `high_risk_reject`: multiple negative/high-confidence reviews or one decisive reject with hard-to-address concerns.
- `borderline`: mixed scores, addressable concerns, uncertain AC decision.
- `rebuttal_sensitive`: concerns are concrete and fixable; strong rebuttal may matter.
- `weak_accept_unstable`: positive but fragile reviews; avoid creating new doubt.
- `split_review`: reviewers disagree sharply; AC summary may matter if rules allow.
- `unknown`: insufficient score/confidence/comment evidence.

Always cite reviewer evidence for posture classification.
