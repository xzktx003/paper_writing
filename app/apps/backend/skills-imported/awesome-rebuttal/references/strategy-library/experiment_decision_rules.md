# Experiment Decision Rules

Classify experiments by decision impact and feasibility.

## Must-do

- Directly addresses high-severity, high-decision-relevance concern.
- Feasible before deadline.
- Result can be explained concisely under venue rules.
- Negative result risk is acceptable or informative.

## High-value optional

- Helpful for credibility but not central to decision.
- Feasible if time remains.
- May be deferred if writing/rule constraints dominate.

## Not realistic or unsuitable

- Too expensive for rebuttal window.
- Requires unavailable data/compute.
- Not allowed by venue rules.
- Weakly linked to reviewer concern.
- Would create more questions than it resolves.
