## Compression rules

- Merge repeated concerns across reviewers.
- Prioritize major/common/decision-critical issues.
- Use compact tables only when they save space and numbers are verified.
- Do not duplicate reviewer-by-reviewer prose unless required by format.
- Replace long apology with short acknowledgment + evidence.
- Keep lower-priority fixes to one sentence or defer intentionally.
